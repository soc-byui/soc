Our Mission

The Security Operations Center (SOC) is the front line of defense in today’s digital battlefield. Our mission is to safeguard Rexburg City's critical systems, investigate threats, and train a new wave of Cybersecurity defenders.

By joining our program, you will gain hands-on experience with real tools, incidents, and teamwork preparing you for the challenges of a Tier 1 SOC Analyst role.

The SOC Training Path

Complete the training shown below, then move into a real SOC to put your knowledge into action. Through this program, you will gain resume-ready Tier 1 SOC Analyst experience.

  • Step 1: Networking Fund.
    • TCP/IP Model
    • OSI Model
    • Flags (SYN/ACK/FIN)
    • DNS
  • Step 2: Networking Cont.
    • Common Ports
    • Subnetting
    • Internal VS. External IP Addresses
    • RFC Editor
  • Step 3: TCP Fund.
    • TCP Sequencing Numbers
    • TCP Windowing (Sliding Window)
    • IP Fragmentation and Offsets
    • TCP Headers and Sizes
  • Step 4: Detection
    • Malware Types
    • Indicators of Compromise
    • Command-Line Fundamentals
    • Alert and Rule Creation
  • Step 5: Hands-On Labs
    • Junior Security Analyst Intro
    • Intro to SIEMs
    • Traffic Analysis Essentials
    • Intro to Cyber Threat Intel
    • Threat Intel Tools
  • Step 6: Tools of the Trade
    • MITRE ATT&CK
    • Endpoint Detection and Response
    • Ticketing Software
    • PCAP Analysis
  • Step 7: SIEM Navigation
    • Alerts
    • Dashboards
    • Hunt
    • Cases
    • Detections
    • PCAP
    • Third-Party Tools
